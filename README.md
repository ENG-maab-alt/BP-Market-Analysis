BP Financial Performance Analysis (2021–2024)
Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive financial performance and strategic analysis of BP’s stock during the period from 2021 to 2024. The objective is to evaluate price trends, stability, and market dynamics to provide actionable insights for investment decision-making.

Methodology
The analysis was conducted using a systematic data engineering and visualization approach:

Data Collection & Cleaning: Historical stock price time series were processed to derive key metrics such as "Daily Change" and "Volatility Range."

Statistical Analysis: Calculation of adjusted close price means and volatility levels to filter out market noise.

Visualization: Power BI was utilized to build an interactive dashboard, linking price movements with trading volume and quarterly performance.

Key Findings
Sustainable Growth: Analysis shows a consistent upward trajectory in BP’s stock value since 2021, indicating long-term strategic growth.

Shift Towards Stability: After the volatility peak in 2022, the "Volatility Index" shows a clear downward trend, marking a transition to a more stable and predictable trading environment by 2024.

Market Activity: A positive correlation between trading volume and price spikes confirms that price momentum is supported by robust institutional demand.

Conclusion & Recommendations
BP’s stock demonstrates resilient growth and reduced risk. Based on the analysis, the stock presents an attractive profile for investors seeking sustainable returns. It is recommended to maintain the dashboard for quarterly updates to monitor future shifts in market volatility.
